First task! When you have a chance, familiarize yourself with the Zak The Baker website.
Here you can find information and media coverage of how Zak The Baker has grown over the years and how his product is different from others.

  • These resources are for employees that work for both the Deli and the Bakery locations
  • Bakery employees that are hired as "Drivers" or employees hired for night shifts will not require parking
  • Full Time employees are eligible for insurance after the 90-day probationary period
Passing the 90-day probationary period means the employee is always:
  1. On Time
  2. On task
  3. Communicates well with others and with their supervisors
  4. Delivers excellent customer service at all times
You will be subject to a 90-day performance review where you will be told where your strengths and weaknesses are, this review can decide whether your employment with the company continues or not.

If you have any questions or concerns don't hesitate to contact the office Administrator Anyully Rosales who is available Monday-Friday from 9am until 5pm.
